HSP / Scent Sensitivity Strategy

Best bets for low-fragrance environments:

  1. 1 Furnished Finder ADU/cottage/bungalow listings — Standalone units, not cleaned by hotel housekeeping.
  2. 2 SabbaticalHomes — Actual people's homes. Minimal vacation-rental "staging scents."
  3. 3 Landing — Apartment-style, professional but not hotel-level fragrancing.
  4. 4 Airbnb Superhosts with 4.8+ ratings — More attentive to guest requests. Message before booking.
  5. 5 Cal Mar Hotel Suites — Private entrance suites, apartment-style. Call ahead about cleaning products.
